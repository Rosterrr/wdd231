Site Name

Bitcoin's Future

This name represents a platform that educates users on Bitcoin's potential benefits for the future and its role in the global economy.

Site Purpose

The site will provide information about the future benefits of Bitcoin, how it could impact global finance, and why it could become a cornerstone of digital economies. It will include articles, data visualizations, and expert opinions on the topic.

Scenarios

  • What are the long-term financial benefits of Bitcoin?
  • How will Bitcoin influence global economies in the next decade?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #003366 (used for headers, footers, and primary text).
  • Accent Color: #ff9900 (used for calls to action, links, and emphasis on important content).

Typography

  • Font 1: Arial, sans-serif (used for body text).
  • Font 2: 'Georgia', serif (used for headings and emphasized text).

Wireframe

Mobile View: The wireframe should consist of a navigation bar at the top with three links: Home, Articles, Contact. Below that, a large hero section with an introduction to Bitcoin. Below the hero, there should be three sections about Bitcoin’s future benefits, news, and community insights. Each section should have brief text and a call to action.

Desktop View: The wireframe for desktop will include a similar structure but with additional content areas like a sidebar for recent news and articles. The main content area should display articles in a grid format, and the footer will have detailed contact and social media links.

Testing

Check and evaluate the HTML using tools like W3C Validator for HTML and CSS, WebAIM's accessibility checker, and Lighthouse for performance and SEO optimization.
